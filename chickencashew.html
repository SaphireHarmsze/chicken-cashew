Chicken cashew

Ingredients

  • 1-2 dried long red chillies
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 3 tabelspoons chicken or vegetable stock
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 80 g cashew nuts
  • 4-5 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 500 g skinless chicken
  • 1 red capsicum (pepper), cut into strips
  • half a carrot, sliced diagonally
  • 1 small onion, cut into 6 wedges
  • 2 sping onions, cut into 1 cm lentgh
  • ground pepper, to sprinkle

instructions:

Take the stem off the dried chillies, cut each chilli into 1 cm pieses with scissors or sharp knife and discard the seeds

Mix the fish sauce, oyster sauce, stock and sugar in a small bowl.

Stir-fry the oil in a wok over a medium heat and stirfry the cashew nuts for 1 to 3 minutes or until light brown. Remove eith a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.

Add the chillies in the same oil over a medium heat fro a minute. They should darken but not blacken and burn. Remove with a slotted spoon.

Heat the same oil aganin and stir-fry half of the garlic and over a mdeium heat untillight brown. Add half of the chicken pieces and stir-fry over a high heat for 4 to 5 minutes or until the chicken is cooked. Remove fron the wok and repeat with the remaining garlic and chicken pices. return allthe chicken to the wok.

Add the capsicum, carrot, onion and the sauce mixture to the wok and stir0fry for 1 to 2 minutes. Taste, then adjust the seasoning if necesarry. Add the cashew nuts, chillies and spring onions and toss well. Sprinkle with ground white pepper.
