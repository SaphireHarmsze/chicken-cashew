Thai cooking ingredients

Fish sauce

Fish sauce is a sauce made from fermented fish.
The term describes a wide variety of products used in different cultures and times.
Some fish sauces are made from raw fish, others from dried fish, others from shrimp, crab or squid

Curry paste

Thai curries are always made with a curry paste.
Common ingredients are: Shrimp paste,Chillies; depending on the curry these can be dried or fresh, red or green
Onions or shallots, Garlic, Lemongrass, Galangal, Coriander (cilantro) root

Coconut milk

Coconut milk, is an opaque, milky white liquid obtained from the germ white of mature coconuts.
Coconut milk has a matte texture and a rich taste due to its high coconut oil content.

Pandan rice

Pandan or Jasmin rice is grown primarily in Thailand
It is moist and soft in texture when cooked, with a slightly sweet flavor.
The grains cling and are somewhat sticky when cooked.

Chillies

Thai chili is a chili pepper, Cultivated in Southeast Asia.
It is widely used in many Asian cuisines.

Black pepper

Black peppercorns were the original source of heat in Thai food before the arrival of chiles from the Americas in the 16th century.
They're part of traditional marinades for grilled chicken and grilled beef.

limes

Lime juice gives a tart lift to grilled meats, salads and fried rice.
Lime leaves are often used in Thai cooking, but if they're hard to find use freshly grated lime zest to give a similar floral,
citrusy aroma to curries and soups.

Shallots & Garlic

There are different types of shallots with various shapes and colors.
Unlike the onion, the shallot forms clusters of tubers, just like garlic.
Garlic is Like onions and chives,a bulbous plant that stores its energy in a bulb
and can grow and bloom very quickly. The plant grows in well-drained soil and in full sun.

Fresh herbs

Generous use of fresh herbs like cilantro, mint, Thai basil add distinctive flavors to everything from salads to curries to fried rice.
Thai basil has a sweet anise flavor. Use it if you can find it, otherwise substitute regular basil.
